## Key Takeaways for Exam Preparation

1. **~90% of questions are IDENTICAL** between 2025 and 2026 — the 2025 answers are directly usable
2. **Game Theory is expanded** in 2026 — new questions on agent assumptions and mixed strategies
3. **Language modeling (Elman/NNets)** questions are NOT listed in 2026 prep — but the lecture exists, so be prepared anyway
4. **Two new lectures** in 2026: Introduction (Lecture 1) and Humane CogSci (Lecture 15) — easy questions, focus on conceptual framing
5. **Lecture ordering changed** but content is the same — don't be confused by numbering
6. **The 2025 answers document is your best study resource** — it has full written answers to almost every question you'll face
